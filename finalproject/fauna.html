------------------PUFFINS------------------
If you travel to Iceland during the summer, you have a high chance of spotting puffins! Puffins are common all over Iceland during the summer season and the early or late shoulder seasons such as May or September.

Price per tour: 40USD

-------------ARTIC FOXES--------------
In the winter, these foxes have snowy white coats to help them blend into the Arctic wilderness. In the summer, foxes in Iceland turn a brown-black color. If you plan to visit the Icelandic highlands, you have a higher chance of spotting Arctic Foxes in Iceland as there is less traffic and the area is more remote.

Price per tour: 90USD
