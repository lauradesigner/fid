---------SELJALANDSFOSS WATERFALL---------
Seljalandsfoss is one of the best-known waterfalls in Iceland. It is approximately 60 meters tall and it is just breathtakingly. It is a must when visiting the south coast, you will not regret it!

Price per tour: 50USD

-------------RIDE HORSES--------------
The Icelandic horse has become popular for being easy-going and one of the friendliest horses in the world. In Iceland, they are usually treated as a member of the family and have plenty of space in huge fields. The horses can live up to 40 years. Icelandic horses are known to be extremely versatile, good-tempered and intelligent.

Price per ride: 90USD
