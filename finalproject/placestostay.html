PLACES TO STAY

----------------EYE RESORT-----------------
Staying at this beautiful hotel you will enjoy the marvellous sunrise mornings, surrounded by diversity of sea animals. During the day, you can enjoy one of the many tours this hotel offers such as kayak, flyboard, scuba diving and so much more.

Price per night: 130USD

-------------HOT SPRINGS HOTEL-------------
Another way to enjoy Iceland to the fullest is by staying at one of the most amazing hot springs. This hotel offers 24/7 access to the hot springs, you can order drinks and the waiters will be more than happy to serve you.

Price per night: 170USD
